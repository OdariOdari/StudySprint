About StudySprint

StudySprint is a productivity tool to help you focus, track, and improve study habits. Whether for exams, skills, or projects, StudySprint simplifies session management.

Key Features

  • Timer: Track time with a stopwatch to stay focused.
  • Past Sessions: Review sessions for consistency.
  • Progress Tracking: Visualize study hours.
  • Achievements: Unlock badges for milestones.
  • Light/Dark Mode: Switch themes for comfort.

Our Mission

StudySprint empowers learners with discipline and productivity. Small efforts lead to big results, and we're here to support you.
